Our paper entitled [“Generative Recommendation with Semantic IDs: A Practitioner’s Handbook”](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.22224) (in short GRID) has been accepted to CIKM 25. GRID is one of the first few public resources that produce state-of-the-art performance using generative recommendation models. Please check out GRID at [our public repo](https://github.com/snap-research/GRID)!
