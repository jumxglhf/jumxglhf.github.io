Two papers about RecSys are accepted to WWW 25 and selected as oral presentations. [One](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.23300) studies the learning dynamic of RecSys models from the perspective of matrix rank to accelerate the training process; [the other](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.17245) studies RecSys model parameter reduction using graph-based hashing methods. Congrats to [Donald](https://www.donaldloveland.com/) and [Xinyi](https://xinyiwu98.github.io/)!
